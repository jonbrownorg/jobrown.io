About Color Printing
Our color printing service is one of our most popular. We can take a pre-designed business card and print it out for you at a very minimal cost. Brochures, Stationary, and even catalogs. Anything you need, photos and more. Usually the color printing service is bundled in with a design project but if you already have the files or an original of the file then we can print it for you.

Four Color Presses
We do not use a color photo copier, please keep this in mind when you ask for this service. We do send this to our press department and they do run these professionally through a press for the highest quality. Because we do this the minimum order you can buy is 50ct and the most is 10,000ct for any one item. It also takes about a week to a week and a half to process this so if you need something rushed then you will enjoy our other service.

Rush Service
We can rush your order by printing out 100ct color prints on a high quality color laser printer this is only done for people who are waiting on a print job from the press department. it is an additional charge of 80.00 but its worth it if you need it now.

What software do you use?
We use Adobe Pressready and Adobe Photoshop. If you have photos or files try to give them to us in an Adobe or Macromedia file format.
