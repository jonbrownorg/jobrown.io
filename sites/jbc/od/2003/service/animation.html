About Animation
Animation, is the process by which inanimate objects can move. We take animation a step further, and use it as a method to help get your message out to the world. There are many different types of animation solutions that you can use but which one is the best for you.

Web Animation
Web animation is best for the client that wants something eye catching for the web or for their web site. This will be a fast loading and easy to view medium with maximum user viewability because it will be on the internet. However this medium is only for the internet and powerpoint. It cant be used for any television or broadcasting presentations.

Computer Animation
We offer computer animation for those who are looking for a large and high quality presentation but aren't necessarily going to put it on the internet. This is ideal for the client that wants to incorporate their animation with a commercial or some form of television and broadcasting presentation.

We Can meet your needs.
We can give you the best of both worlds and create custom animation for both your web page and for a commercial, but it will take longer to assure that the best quality will be met.

What software do you use?
We use Macromedia Flash and Director for web animations and presentations. We use Bryce, Maya, and Lightwave for computer animation. We diversify to give you the best quality animation's possible
