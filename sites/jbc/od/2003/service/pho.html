About Photo Printing
Our photo printing takes no time at all with our state of the art scanners we can scan your print or negative at such a high resolution that when we print out your print on kodak photo glossy paper you wont be able to tell the difference between a darkroom copy and our printout. If you would rather have a custom darkroom enlargement we can also accommodate you there. We can print on a variety of different mediums, paper, banners and signs just tell us what you need and we will make it for you.

Digital Photo Prints
We do also specialize in digital photo printing. We can take your photos either on scan disk, flash card, floppy, zip or CD and turn your digital album into a real set of prints. At less than 15 cents per photo you will have the knowledge that your photos are quality. If you want a photo altered then we can also do that too.

Photo Alterations
As mentioned above we do alter photographs. We can brighten, lighten, take out obstructions in the photo and correct red eye. Just tell us what you need and we can help you.

What software do you use?
We use Adobe Photoshop and Corel Photo Paint. If you have pictures just drag them from your digital camera onto a floppy, zip, or CD and we can print them out for you.
