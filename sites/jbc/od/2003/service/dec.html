About Decals
Decals can be used for any product. Use a decal to put your company logo on your product or use them as stickers to seal envelopes. We also create decals for vehicles. Vinal decals for glass office doors also create a professional look.

Sickers
Stickers are useful as give aways also to put a quick logo on a folder or company car. Our stickers come in sheets of 30 (small stickers), 15 (medium stickers) and 8 large stickers. All stickers are printed on 8.5 X 11 sticker paper.

What software do you use?
We use Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator. If you have photos or files try to give them to us in an Adobe or Macromedia file format.
