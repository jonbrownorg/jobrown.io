The Flip Side Special Chi Episode

 Add this to -

January 1st, 2007
On The Flip Side : Special Chi Edition for 1.1.2007
By: Jon Brown
Rating : ” ”

“Today, were talking with the creator of the Chi Widget, its a really great widget that allows people to have more fun in their dashboard. Its a growing plant widget for your dashboard. Based on the real plant the Chia Pet, the widget will grow on your computer every 3 days. But like a real plant, it needs water and sunlight (not really). When clicking the water droplet button, it feeds your pet for 3 days. A fun idea coupled with great graphics makes the Chi Pet a great widget. There is a beta program for this widget which can be found at Novis Design.”
