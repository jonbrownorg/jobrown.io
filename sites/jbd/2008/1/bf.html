Experienced Cocoa Programmer

We are trying to port our OSX (Calorie Tracker) widget into a program. It queries a database online and lists a set of results, when each result is clicked it shows the details of that result. Pretty easy, also looking to incorporate a few other features. This is a health program and the database is a list of over 75000 food items. When the search takes place it searches and lists the food items. When the food item is clicked it shows all the nutrition facts for that food item just like you would see on the actual food item in the store. It then shows a graph below in percentages rather than MG or G. Once chosen the food would be added to a food calculator which would be able to calculate (add) all food items together to spit out a total fat, cals, etc... with graph. This would be able to be exported to excel, text file or printed and saved as a proprietary file for the program .dat for example. Give me your quote!

Dog Year Calculator 1.0

Pig Latin Translator 1.0

Pig Latin Translator is a Dashboard widget that allows you to enter any text and have it translated into pig latin. Simply enter the text into the speech bubble and click the pig to translate.

This widget simply calcuates the population of the world. It takes into consideration death rates and birth rates. Its a fun widget to watch the population of the earth increase!
