Nutrition tracking.

Custom Foods

Add custom food items to the BeFit database and easily manage your custom food entries. Submit foods for distribution to all BeFit users.

Dietary Allowance

Track dietary needs with this feature that calculates your dietary allowance based on your set caloric goals.

Improved Graphing

With our all new graphing UI you can view multiple graphs that track your caloric goals, food list totals and the individual nutrition data for each food.



Manage your caloric goals.

BMI Recommendations

Let BeFit help you customize your diet and recipies with its built in BMI caloric intake recommendation tool, integrates your recomended intake into all areas for easy tracking.

Food Ratings

You now can see at a glance from the food search screen which foods are healthier for you based on the star rating system.

Food Search

Not only is searching simple but with the inclusion of the USDA database and our public foods database it will be hard not to find the food your looking for.

 

More Features

 

Edit food data

You now can edit the food data of the food items you add to your custom food library.

Goal Graphing

View your list caloric totals against your caloric goals to see how your stacking up.

iCloud Sync

You will be able to sync your data in the BeFit app for Mac with Apples iCloud or Dropbox service, coming soon. In app purchase may apply.

Food Lists

Create and manage custom food lists that let you create powerful shopping lists, recipe lists and more.

User Food Library

All foods added will be curated in an easy to manage user food library that you can quickly and easily search, access and edit.

MacOS to iOS Sync

You will soon be able to sync the data on your BeFit for Mac app with the BeFit for iOS app, coming soon. In app purchase may apply.
