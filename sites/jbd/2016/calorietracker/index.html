Calorie Tracker Features

 

Food Search

Not only is searching simple but you have the option of searching our new public database comprised with new foods added by our users daily or the USDA database.

Dietary Allowance

Let the Calorie Tracker keep track of your dietary needs with this new feature that calculates your dietary allowance based on your diet.

Guiding Stars

You now can see at a glance from the food search screen which foods are healthier for you based on the star rating system.

Food Lists

Managing your food list is easy, especially now that your food list data is stored in the cloud, access it anywhere, on any computer where Calorie Tracker is setup.

Results

To view the food graphs search for a food and then select the food you would like to view. You can visualize your caloric intake very quickly.

Export Data

Easily export your food lists to text files for use with other programs. Also you can now export your food lists as a .csv file.
