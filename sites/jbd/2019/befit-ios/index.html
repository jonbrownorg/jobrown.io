BeFit for iOS

BeFit is the ultimate calorie tracking tool. Based on the nutrition data for the foods that you eat. On Sale now for a limited time only!

Available on the iTunes Store

Finally, food tracking made easy.

BMI Recommendations

Let BeFit for iOS help you customize your diet and recipies with its built in BMI caloric intake recommendation tool, integrates your recomended intake into all areas for easy tracking.

Food Library

With a built in library of over 7500 USDA foods and the option to add an additional 54,000 more foods a shortage of nutritional data will never stop you from your weight loss goals.

Food Search

Not only is searching simple but with the inclusion of the USDA database and our public foods database it will be hard not to find the food your looking for.



Missing a food? Scan, add, track, done.

Food Scanner

Adding a custom food item is as easy as point and shoot. Take a picture of a barcode and let BeFit automatically populate a new food item with accurate nutritional data all at no additional fee.

User Food Lists

Create and manage custom food lists that let you create powerful shopping lists, recipe lists and more.

User Food Library

All foods added will be curated in an easy to manage user food library that you can quickly and easily search, access and edit.

 

More Features

 

Nutrition Graphing

With our all new graphing UI you can view multiple graphs that track your caloric goals.

Food Tracker

The food tracker allows you to track your daily, weekly, monthly and annual caloric intake against your goals.

iCloud Sync

You will be able to sync your data in the BeFit app for Mac with Apples iCloud or Dropbox service, coming soon. In app purchase may apply.

Daily Intake

Track nutritional data on a daily basis, and by quantity so you can get a fuller picture of your overall health.

Nutrition Data

The food database calculates and presents nutritional data in an easy to read screen that highlights commonly high and harmful substances like sodium.

MacOS to iOS Sync

You will soon be able to sync the data on your BeFit for Mac app with the BeFit for iOS app, coming soon. In app purchase may apply.
