About me
My life
I am an avid traveler and Ive sen lots of asia. I love going and seeing new things. I am a thinker, I really enjoy sitting and talking with people about things that i am or have thought about. I like laid back people with a good sense of humor. I am currently writing a book about my life in hopes that I will be able to inspire someone out there to do something extraordinary. I believe that everyone has the capacity for doing something great but its our own lack of inspiration and motivation that keeps us from being and doing what we want. I do not consider my life in any way special or great but I do believe that I am living and that I am doing something great with my life.

Nice guy
Sometimes I feel like I am one of the few nice guys left out there. I am not an average guy and I have been making lots of discoveries lately about what it means to be a man and what kinds of characteristics I want and need to have in order to be the person I want to be. I believe that some Men give us a bad name and I want to try to break through those typical stereotypes in order to show people that there are a few of us guys who truly admire people for who they are and not what they appear to be on the outside.

Ethics
I would say that I have strong personal and social ethics that I always try to keep to. I am not a man prone to fits of rage or uncontrollable anger. I believe that patience is the key when dealing with the problem. I always think that honesty is the best way to go because I just dont have the energy to keep up with lies. I think a person who is honest with other people can be honest with himself. I always try to be a helpful person and what I would consider a team player. I am very caring and sincere yet I always am on task to see that things get done.
