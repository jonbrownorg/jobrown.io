Play more games in class.
Studies have proven that having fun in the classroom actually improves the students academic participation. There are several games on the site to use, that are proven to work wonderfully for any age range. We have a great list of games available right here check them out! Learn Some Games!

Sing Songs
When you were a kid, you sang songs, made chants, and said funny limericks. Teach some of these to your students, ie: Row Row Row your Boat, Mary had a little lamb, Its raining its pouring.

Worksheets, Worksheets, Worksheets!
Use worksheets and handouts in the class. Having the kids look at pictures, and make there own sentences heightens their ability to speak and understand english. We have a wide variety of ESL worksheets available right here! Download Worksheets Now!

Repeat Often!
Whether your teaching adults, children or teenagers, the key to successful teaching is repetition. Keep repeating and reviewing, mix it up play games or do something to make learning fun!

Practice Dialogues
There is no better way to teach valuable dialogues than to use them in your class. By practicing dialogues you offer the student a head start in understanding valuable grammar in the future. You can try two methods to implement this into your class. One way is to have the student make their own dialogue comprised of already taught grammar, or you the teacher prepare a dialogue with new grammar that will lead into another point of your less.

Mock Talent Shows
Be the star teacher at your school for this idea. Have your students bit by bit work on some english plays, skits, or dialogues to preform for the school or just for the other students in the class. The work and dedication that it will take to prepare will be just what you need to have them motivated to learn new topics and grammar. This is a great way to inspire and make the classroom both challenging and fun.
