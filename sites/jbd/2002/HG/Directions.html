Here are the Directions to Oak Park, Greenfield NH Heading from the East


1. Take 101 E. to Wilton, NH

2. Take 31 N. into Greenfield

3. You'll come down a hill into the center of Greenfield, past the Greenfield Inn B&B and the library on you left. Rt. 31 merges with Rt. 136 S. The large, handsome Greenfield Meeting House will be on a small knoll on your right.

4. Continue going straight on Rt 136 S.; dont take the right turn (just past the meeting house) which would keep you on Rt. 31

5. Bear right at the fork (leave 136-follow the signs to Greenfield State Park). Oak Park is just ahead on your left. Greenfield State Park is across the road on the right.




From the west (Keene, NH)

1. Take 101 E. to Peterborough, NH

2. You'll come down a long hill to the only stoplight in Peterborough. It's at the junction of Rts. 101 and 202

3. Turn left onto 202 N. and follow that approx. 8 miles. You'll start up a hill with a passing lane in the northbound lane. At the crest of the hill is a flashing yellow light suspended over 202. Turn right at that light onto Forest Road.

4. Continue on Forest Road (through a covered bridge) 4 miles until you come to Oak Park, which will be on your right. If you go into the center of Greenfield, you've gone a bit too far.




From the North (Concord, NH):

1. From I-89, take exit 5 onto Rts. 9 and 202 S.

2. After you go through downtown Hillsborough, you'll pass McDonald's (on your right). At the stoplight there, Rt. 9 continues straight, and 202 bears left.

3. Turn left onto 202 S.

4. Follow 202 S. (you'll pass through Antrim, NH).

5. Approx. 5 miles south of Antrim, you'll start up a long hill. There's a flashing yellow light suspended over the road at the crest of that hill.

6. Turn left at that light/intersection. You will be on Forest Road.

7. Continue on Forest Road (you'll pass through a covered bridge) 4 miles until you come to Oak Park, which will be on your right. If you come to the center of Greenfield, you've gone a bit too far.




From the South:

Directions from the south depend on where you pick up Rt. 101 in New Hampshire.

If you're coming from the Boston area, take 3 N to Nashua. Pick up 101A W, and follow the "From the East" directions.

If you're coming from central Mass., taking 202 N through Winchendon, MA, just follow the "From the West" directions once you go through Peterborough, NH.

If you're coming from western Mass., up I91 N, go to Keene, NH, pick up 101 E, and follow the "From the West" directions.
