Hi, I’m Jon and I’m a coach & mentor

From 2014-2022 I was a trusted voice, advisor and mentor to many. To my clients I was a steady hand offering sound advice on how to use technology in the workplace in a trusted manner. During this time I leaned on and learned from my journey as business owner operator in many forms. Early on I worked with a firm to build a brand and learned the power of storytelling, core values and trust building.

This combined with my work ethic and past life experiences put me in a role where I can start helping others down their entrepreneurial path and journey. I am not a head in the clouds style coach and mentor, my tactical and brass tacks approach to business means that if you're really ready to take that first step, I will help get take that step and thrive.
