Frequently Asked Questions

Where does the seafood information come from?

Seafood Guide uses data from the Monterey Bay Aquarium Seafood Watch program. Recommendations are based on scientific research that considers fish population health, environmental impact, and fisheries management practices.

Does the app require an internet connection?

An internet connection is not required for basic lookups and reference information once the app is installed. This makes it useful in grocery stores, markets, and restaurants where connectivity may be limited.

What does “sustainable” mean in the app?

Sustainability ratings take into account how a seafood species is harvested or farmed, its population status, environmental impact, and long-term viability. The app simplifies this information into clear, consumer-friendly guidance.

Does the app cover invasive species?

Yes. Seafood Guide identifies invasive species where applicable and provides guidance to help users understand their environmental impact and consumption considerations.

Is this app intended for professionals or consumers?

Both. Seafood Guide is designed to be approachable for everyday consumers while still providing accurate, well-sourced information that food professionals, educators, and sustainability advocates can trust.
