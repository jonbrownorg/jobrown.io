Features

Comprehensive Nutrition Database

Access over 54,000 food items sourced from the USDA, with detailed macro and micronutrient breakdowns for accurate tracking and analysis.

Personalized Calorie Goals

Automatically calculate recommended daily caloric intake based on your profile, activity level, and goals using built-in BMI and BMR calculations.

Food Logging & Meal Tracking

Track individual foods or entire meals and see how each contributes to your daily, weekly, and monthly nutrition goals.

Custom Foods & Sharing

Create your own food items when something isn’t in the database and share custom entries across your library for consistent tracking.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is BeFit designed for?

BeFit is designed for anyone who wants to track calorie intake based on a defined dietary allowance, whether your goal is weight loss, maintenance, or better nutritional awareness.

Where does the nutrition data come from?

All built-in food data is sourced from the USDA nutrition database, providing detailed and reliable nutritional information across thousands of foods.

Can I track more than just calories?

Yes. BeFit tracks macro and micronutrients, allowing you to see nutrient levels for individual foods as well as combined meals over time.

Is BeFit free to use?

Yes. BeFit is 100% free and does not require a subscription to access its core features.
