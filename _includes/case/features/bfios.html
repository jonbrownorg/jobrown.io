Features

Barcode Food Scanner

Add foods instantly by scanning barcodes with your iPhone camera. BeFit automatically creates food entries with accurate nutritional data, eliminating manual input and guesswork.

Smart Food Search

Search across USDA datasets and public food libraries to quickly find what you need. Can’t find a food? Add it once and track it forever.

Personalized Calorie Targets

BeFit calculates recommended calorie intake using BMI-based guidance and integrates those targets throughout the app, keeping your tracking aligned with your goals.

Food Lists & Libraries

Create custom food lists for shopping, meal planning, or recipes. All saved foods are organized into a personal library that’s easy to search, edit, and reuse.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is BeFit for iOS different from the Mac version?

BeFit for iOS is designed for quick, on-the-go tracking with features like barcode scanning, mobile-optimized graphs, and fast food entry. It complements the Mac version rather than replacing it.

Do I need to manually enter nutrition information?

No. Most foods can be added via search or barcode scan. BeFit automatically fills in nutritional data using USDA and public food databases.

Can I track calories over time?

Yes. BeFit lets you track daily, weekly, monthly, and yearly calorie intake, helping you understand long-term trends and progress toward your goals.

Is BeFit suitable for casual tracking or long-term goals?

Both. BeFit works equally well for quick daily tracking or long-term nutrition planning, giving you flexibility without overwhelming complexity.
