Frequently Asked Questions

How is Animal Tracker different from the macOS version?

Animal Tracker for iOS is designed for quick, visual comparisons on your iPhone. It focuses on touch-friendly graphs, fast conversions, and mobile workflows rather than large data exports or enterprise use cases.

Does the app account for different animal breeds?

Yes. When breed-specific lifespan data is available, Animal Tracker adjusts age conversions and lifespan expectations accordingly to provide more accurate results.

Are the lifespan values estimates or exact numbers?

All lifespan values are estimates based on commonly accepted averages and research data. Individual animals may age differently depending on health, environment, and care.
